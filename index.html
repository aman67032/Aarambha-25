About Aarambh 2025

Aarambh 2025 is the annual orientation program of JK Lakshmipat University designed to welcome new students to the campus and introduce them to university life. The week-long program includes interactive sessions, workshops, cultural performances, and fun activities that help freshers adjust to the new environment and make lasting connections.

This year's theme focuses on innovation, sustainability, and building a community of future leaders. Join us for an exciting journey that marks the beginning of your university experience!

Events & Activities

Ice Breaking Sessions

Fun activities to help freshers get to know each other and form new friendships.

Expert Talks

Inspirational speeches from industry leaders and academia to motivate students.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on various skills and technologies relevant to your field of study.

Cultural Night

Showcase your talent and enjoy performances by fellow students and professionals.

Sports & Games

Recreational activities and friendly competitions to promote team spirit.

Explore the university campus, facilities, and resources available to students.
